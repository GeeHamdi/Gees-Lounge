A note from the author

I write about the things people often feel but struggle to explain: manipulation, emotional confusion, difficult relationships, hidden motives, resilience, and the peculiar theatre of human behaviour.

These books are not interested in fake positivity. They are interested in recognition, clarity, honest reflection, and perhaps the occasional moment of: “So that is what was happening.”
