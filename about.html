My Approach

Language, psychology, and the person behind the message

For the past 15 years, I have worked with university students, professionals, and executives to help them master the art of communication. Whether they are preparing a high-stakes presentation, developing compelling research, or navigating a fast-paced professional environment, I help them find and strengthen their voice.

Over more than a decade in the classroom, I have learned that communication is never just about words. It is also about how people think, how they feel when they speak, and how confidently they connect with others.

Alongside my Master's degree in TESOL, I hold a diploma in practical psychology. My approach therefore goes beyond grammar and vocabulary. It considers the whole picture: the mindset behind the message, the anxiety surrounding public speaking, the development of genuine professional confidence, and the ability to connect meaningfully with an audience.

Whether we are working on Business English, refining soft skills, or structuring an academic paper, my aim is to provide practical tools that can be used immediately.
